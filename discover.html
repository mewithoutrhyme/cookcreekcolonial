Historic architecture, rolling green farmlands & mountain adventures.

Explore the local towns and villages and experience why Litchfield Hills embodies the New England lifestyle.Litchfield County has so much to discover!

adventure

  • optoutside: Steep Rock Preserve

    Walk, bike or drive to the nearby preserve that boasts almost 1000 acres of protected land. Hike along the river bed, cool off in the Rail Road Tunnel or, for the more adventurous, head up to the summit for breathtaking views of the Shepaug River Valley.

  • hike: Hidden Valley Preserve

    A beautiful, easy hike along the Shepaug River, suitable for all ages and skill levels. The Thoreau Footbridge, located at the start of the trail, is worth the trip alone. (pictured)

  • bike: KC&E Adventures

    Located in nearby New Preston, KC&E offers whole new way to experience Litchfield Hills with self-guided e-bike experiences.

dine

  • The Po Café

    Located in the heart of Washington, CT and listed as a top spot for coffee and a view, The Po Cafe offers a quintessential New England experience (and delicious food to boot).

  • dinner: Community Table

    With a regularly updated menu, Community table sources the closest, freshest ingredients and playfully combines them into unforgettable bites. Farm to table at it's best - reservations highly recommended!

  • The Smithy Café & Store

    Housed in nearby New Preston, The Smithy (as we call it) is both café on one side of the street and local market, on the other. Pickup a premade pizza, quiche or pot-pie, and some local lettuces for those an easy at-home meal.

shop

  • Hickory Stick Book Shop

    Hickory Stick Book Shop is a beloved Litchfield County fixture. Stop by to pick up a vacation read, or for one of their many literary events.

  • Pergola

    A thoughtfully curated collection of items and furnishings that blend nature and home with an artful eye toward weathered design.

  • Five Janes

    A short walk or ride into the heart of The Depot for gifts, clothing & more.

  • Privet House

    Part homegoods store, part design gallery, Privet House is an ecclectic wonderland of timelss high-design finds to make any house, a home.
